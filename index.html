What is Habitus?

Habitus is an innovative habit-tracker that uses your webcam to help you to be healthier at the computer

Image recognition

Habitus uses machine learning algorithms applied to image recognition to interpret your habits from your webcam's captured images

read more

No user input needed

Habitus interprets and stores your habits for you! You don't have to regularly and manually update your information like with typical habit trackers

read more

Everything is local

You don't have to worry about who sees your webcam's images since all the image processing is done locally. No need for them to go up to the cloud!

Amazing Features

Habitus helps you to be healthier at the computer by warning you of your unconcious bad habits and reminding you to engage in new healthier ones

Posture correction

Habitus will warn you whenever your posture is unhealthy for your back

No more nail biting

Habitus will help you stop nail biting and warn you whenever you do it

Water reminder

Are you drinking your daily 2 liters of water? Habitus will help you with that!

Sanitary practices

Habitus will warn if you're touching your face too much, if you're not coughing into your elbow and remind you to wash your hands

Customizable notifications

If you think the notifications are too noisy or you're just not in the mood to be interrupted, just change Habitus preferences!

Statistics

Habitus will show you how many times you've incurred on an habit and how you're improving

Compatible with other apps

You can still use Habitus while using your webcam for Skype, Zoom, Discord, or any other app that uses it

Much more!

Along with these come many more features that will help you achieve a better lifestyle at your computer!
